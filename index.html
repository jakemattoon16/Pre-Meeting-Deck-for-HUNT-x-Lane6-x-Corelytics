About Lane6

Who We Are

Better data.
Same team. Same operation.

Lane6 is a backend operations company. We work inside existing businesses to sharpen the financial and operational reporting those businesses already run — making it more accurate, more current, and more detailed than what they have today.

We are not here to take over your operation. An organization the size of HUNT Real Estate ERA has its own infrastructure, its own leadership, and its own processes — and those stay exactly as they are. What we focus on is the quality of the information those processes produce. Your team keeps running the reports. We make sure those reports are telling the full story.

The gap we consistently find in businesses at your scale is not effort — it's visibility. Leaders are making decisions from data that is delayed, incomplete, or aggregated in ways that obscure what's actually happening underneath. We close that gap without disrupting how the business runs.

"The decisions don't change. The information behind them does."

How We Work

The Engagement Model

Three phases.
One consistent outcome.

Phase 01
Audit

We map where revenue is leaking and where operations are breaking down. Not a generic assessment — a specific diagnosis of your business, your numbers, and your current processes.

Phase 02
Build

We install the systems, workflows, and reporting layer your business actually needs. Built to your operation, not adapted from a template. No unnecessary complexity.

Phase 03
Maintain

We deploy and maintain the Corelytics reporting layer. Your team continues running the business exactly as they do today — with better information to work from. Our role is keeping that reporting accurate, current, and available.

The Reporting Layer

Corelytics

The same reports.
Sharper, faster, more complete.

What it does — and what it doesn't touch.

Corelytics is the reporting layer Lane6 deploys and maintains. It does not replace how your team runs the business. It connects to your existing financials and operational data and produces reporting that is more granular, more current, and more forward-looking than what standard systems output by default.

Your leadership is already reviewing reports. Corelytics means those reports show agent-level productivity trends, division-level margin contribution, pipeline visibility across your six regions, and attachment rate performance across mortgage, title, and insurance — updated on a cadence that actually reflects what's happening in the business right now, not 30 days ago.

You don't change how you work. You get better information to work from.

Agent Productivity Reporting

Production distribution across your agent base — by region, by office, by tenure — so you know exactly where volume is concentrated and where it isn't.

Division Revenue Contribution

Clear visibility into what mortgage, title, and insurance are actually contributing relative to brokerage volume — and where attachment rates have room to move.

Pipeline & Transaction Trend Visibility

Track closed volume, average transaction size, and pipeline health across all six regions on a current basis — not a quarterly lag.

Forward-Looking Financial Reporting

Reporting that shows where margin is going, not just where it's been — built from your actual data, updated on a cadence that supports real decisions.
